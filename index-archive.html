What You'll Learn

Fall Term: Backend

In this term you'll learn:

  • Find out what’s driving your computer (or website) to process information!
  • Learn how to code in Python, starting off with the Core 4 fundamentals
  • Logical approach to problem-solving
  • Weekly activities with a dynamic group of volunteers
  • Build simple games like tic-tac-toe and hangman from scratch

Spring Term: Frontend

In this term you'll learn:

  • HTML - learning to build the blueprint of an app or site
  • CSS - bringing your HTML to life through styling
  • Component-driven development
  • A mobile-first approach to styling
  • Flexbox - putting all your components into place
